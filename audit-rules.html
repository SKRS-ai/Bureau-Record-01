3. Forensic Disqualification (The "AI Slop" Clause)

An asset will be summarily rejected if it fails any of the following technical checks:

  • Neural Drift: Evidence of hallucinations that exceed the creator's documented intent.
  • Data Plagiarism: Direct duplication of training set tokens without transformative manual refactoring.
  • Anonymity: Failure to link the asset to a Ballot-Verified Identity (Philadelphia or Minneapolis hub).
