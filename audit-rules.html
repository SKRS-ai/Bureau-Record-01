Audit Standards

The objective technical criteria utilized by Solomon Kin, LLC to evaluate and certify synthetic collaborative intellectual property.

I. The Tiered Thresholds

Certification is granted based on the **Manual Transformation Delta**—the measurable difference between base-model output and final human-refined work.

Tier Classification Manual Delta Audit Intensity
Tier 1: Assisted 10% — 25% Standard Metadata Scan
Tier 2: Collaborative 25% — 50% Full Manifest Review
Tier 3: Sovereign 50% — 100% Forensic Deep-Audit

II. The 100+ Iteration Rule

To prevent "Autonomous Drift," the Bureau requires a documented **Manifest of Intent**. For Tier 3 Sovereign status, the creator must provide evidence of at least **100 iterative refinements** within the neural collaborative cycle.

  • Proof of Directorship: Evidence that the machine followed human linguistic refinement.
  • Recursive Refactoring: Documentation of rejection/revision cycles.
  • Baseline Comparison: Analysis of the original "Seed" vs. the "Certified Final."

III. Forensic Rejection (Non-Compliance)

An asset will be summarily rejected and the audit fee forfeited if the following "Slop Patterns" are detected:

  • Zero-Refinement Output (One-Prompt Generation).
  • Evidence of Model Hallucination masquerading as Intent.
  • Failure to link to a Ballot-Verified Architect Identity.
