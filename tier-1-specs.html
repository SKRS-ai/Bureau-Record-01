Tier 1: Assisted

The Standard for Non-Transformative Neural Generation.

I. The Doctrine of De Minimis Contribution

Tier 1 Certification is applied to assets where the human architect provided the **Initial Prompt (Seed)** but allowed the machine to execute the majority of the aesthetic and structural labor without significant recursive refactoring.

Threshold: 1—24 Iterative Nodes

Under the **2026 AI Intellectual Property Act**, works with low manual delta are classified as **Public Domain (PD)**. The Bureau verifies the origin of the prompt to protect the creator from "Prompt Injection" theft, but acknowledges the asset itself lacks statutory authorship.

II. Forensic Data Metrics

Tier 1 focuses on **Origin Verification** rather than Transformation Analysis.

III. Commercial Risk Warning

Tier 1 assets are susceptible to wholesale duplication and utilization by third parties. The Bureau’s role in Tier 1 is to provide a **Forensic Timestamp** to prove *who* generated the asset first, even if the asset cannot be fully copyrighted. This is essential for preventing bad-faith actors from claiming your prompt-work as their own Sovereign creation.

  • Theft Prevention: Proves you were the "First to Prompt" in the Public Ledger.
  • NIST Compliance: Meets federal requirements for labeling AI-generated content.
  • Path to Upgrade: Provides a baseline from which you can later perform Tier 2 or Tier 3 refactoring.
