Human Agency Charter

The AI-IPVB is founded on the principle that technology must remain a tool of the human spirit, not a replacement for it. We stand as the forensic shield for the "Human Spark."

The Five Pillars of Digital Sovereignty

ARTICLE I

The Right to Attribution

We believe every creator has an inalienable right to be recognized as the primary architect of their work. The Bureau exists to prevent the **"Neural Erasure"** of human contribution by quantifying the specific manual delta in every collaborative project.

ARTICLE II

Protection of the "Thin Black and Brown Lines"

In the transition to a synthetic economy, marginalized communities in hubs like Philadelphia and Minneapolis face the highest risk of algorithmic extraction. Our ethics mandate prioritizing the verification and protection of IP for those historically excluded from legacy tech infrastructure.

ARTICLE III

Transparency of Intent (NMMMB Compliance)

"The machine produces probability; the human produces intent."

The Bureau requires a minimum 50% transformative threshold for Sovereign status to ensure that **autonomous generation** never masquerades as **human-led creation**. We reject "AI Slop" as a matter of forensic and moral principle.

ARTICLE IV

Non-Custodial Data Ethics

Solomon Kin, LLC operates as a **Managed Logistics Provider**, not an owner of user data. We uphold a strict non-custodial standard where the Bureau records the *proof* of work without ever asserting claim over the *soul* of the work.

ARTICLE V

Architectural Accountability

All Bureau activities are overseen by verified IT professionals and Systems Engineers. We bring the rigor of **Infrastructure Management** to the fluidity of digital art, ensuring that our certifications carry the weight of technical truth.
